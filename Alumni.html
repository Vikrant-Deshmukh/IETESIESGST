How can I get involved?

I'd like to be join the team behind the magic!

Organizing Team Member - Applications are now open and will be closing on March 28th! Apply now by clicking here!

Conference Volunteer - Applications for Conference Volunteers will open in November for our 2021 Conference. Also sign up for our email blasts and follow us on Instagram to stay up to date.

I'm interested in giving a talk!

Students - We host an annual public speaking competition, called Speechcraft, where two students are chosen to give TEDx Talks at our Annual Conference. Our next Speechcraft event will be in the Fall Semester.

Everyone else - Please fill out our speaker nomination form and we'll get back to you!
