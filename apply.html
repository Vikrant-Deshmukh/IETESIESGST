Hi There! We're so glad you're interested in joining our team! We will be opening up applications to join our team soon so follow our Instagram for updates.
In the meantime, take some time out to learn more about who we are and what we have to offer!
We have openings on our Marketing, Technology, Creative, Publicity, Design, and Event Co-ordinator

Who We're Looking For

Marketing

Position: Marketing Co-ordinator
Description:
As part of the marketing team you will be in charge of Generating funds for IETE Events! We focus on outreach, generate content for all of the social media platforms (LinkedIn,Facebook, Instagram, etc.), and plan and prepare interactives for our annual events.

Tech Team

Position: Tech Co-ordinator
Description:
As part of the technology team you are in charge of what our audiences see! You will be responsible for maintaining the live website of IETE, and work on projects to improve team efficiency and event experience!
Nice to Haves:
Prior experience with web design, UI/UX, front-end development and projects to showcase familiarity with design and content organization, ability to work under pressure/time constraints, communication and organizational skills.

Publicity

Position: Publicity Co-ordinator
Description:
As a Publicity co-ordinator you will work along with our Publicity heads to promote our events on various social media and bring in participants and attendees for our events.
Nice to Haves:
Analytical skills, budgeting experience, collaborative mindset, communication and public speaking skills.

Design

Position: Visual Designer
Description: You’ll create expressive and communicative graphics and print materials for our events and outreach--posters, program booklets, social media graphics, and whatever else you can imagine! Most importantly, you’ll play a big part in shaping IETESIESGST’ visual identity!
Main Qualifications:
Enthusiastic about creating expressive and effective graphics and print materials for IETESIESGST’ events.
Please Submit:
We’d love to see some of your visual work--a link to your Instagram, Tumblr, blog, portfolio site, or a PDF, whatever format works for you! (Important Note: Resume is not necessary as your portfolio is more important. Also make sure to set your Instagram (if shared) to public so that your content is viewable!)

Event Experience

Position: Event Heads
Description:
You’ll work with the team to organize and oversee the spaces and experience of our events, such as the annual Techxter & Ideation competitions and other smaller ones. How will attendees be directed as they arrive?
How will the venue be organized and decorated? These are some of the questions that you'll answer by working with the team to gather ideas and turning them into detailed and delightful plans. IETE is ultimately about its events, and you’ll play a major part in figuring out making those experiences enjoyable and enlightening.
Nice-To-Haves: If you have some creative or curatorial work, we’d love to see it! They can be anything from drawings to DIY projects to a cool pinterest board.

Creative Team

Position: Creative Co-ordinator
Description:
How will the venue be decorated? How will you make our event standout? These are some of the questions that you'll answer by working with the team.to gather ideas and turning them into detailed and delightful art creations. IETE is ultimately about its events, and you’ll play a major part in figuring out making those experiences enjoyable and enlightening.
