About IETE

The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE) is India's leading recognised professional society devoted to the advancement of Science and Technology of Electronics, Telecommunication & IT. Founded in 1953.

The IETE is the National Apex Professional body of Electronics and Telecommunication, Computer Science and IT Professionals.t serves more than 1,25,000 members (including Corporate, Student and ISF members) through various 64 Centres, spread all over India and abroad. The Institution provides leadership in Scientific and Technical areas of direct importance to the national development and economy. Government of India has recognised IETE as a Scientific and Industrial Research Organization (SIRO) and also notified as an educational Institution of national eminence.

ISF - Students' Forum

The major events held under the banner of ISF are Techxter A National Level Technical Paper Presentation Competition;
Ideation An idea presentation competition; Bootcamps on various topics; Seminars- on upcoming technologies. ISF organized an Idea presentation competition n name ideas in collaboration with various NGOs. In this competition, students were given various problem statements pertaining to various social issues faced by them. ISF also conducted a bootcamp to ISME, One Indiabulls, Parel. Topics such as Data Science, Design Thinking etc. were discussed in this bootcamp by experts from various fields. Apart from competitions, ISF organizes workshops on trending technologies to expose students to concepts and tools apart from the prescribed syllabus. Workshops such as GitHub, ML, Python etc. were conducted.
