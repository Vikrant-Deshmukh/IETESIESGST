Our Events

Techxter

Date: February 29nd, 2020 | Theme: Everyday Technologies

TECHXTER is a National Level Technical Paper Presentation (TPP), open to all the branches of engineering and polytechnic. This event is held by the IETE Students’ Forum (ISF) of SIES Graduate School of Technology, Nerul. After eight previous successes, TECHXTER 9.0 marked the ninth consecutive year of Techxter. This event was open to students of all branches of engineering, polytechnic, and other technical courses. We received over 53 papers from colleges across India such as Sheshgiri College of Engineering and Technology, Kolhapur Institute of Technology, etc. from various departments such as Information Technology, Computer Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Printing and Packaging Technology, Biotechnology, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering etc.

Ideation

Date: September 29nd, 2019

Ideation, an ideathon, was a collaborative event which was organized by the combined efforts of IETE Students’ Forum and EDC of SIES Graduate School of Technology. The aim of the competition was to provide a medium for the enthusiasts to generate creative and technically productive ideas to real life problems faced by people in the society. The event began with the Inauguration ceremony, in the presence of our Guest of Honor Dr. B. U. Rindhe, Chairman, IETE, Navi Mumbai Centre, and Principal, Dr. Atul Kemkar. It was organized in collaboration with two NGOs, Blue Ribbon Movement and Child Vision Foundation. The problem statements were given by two NGO partners which are community issues such as waste management, water management, road safety, robbery, issues faced by the differently abled etc.

Projexions: Project Competition

Date: October 23rd & 24th, 2019| Theme: IOT & Andriod App Development

The IETE Students’ Forum and the Department of Information Technology conducted PROJEXIONS, a project exhibition for the Third Year and Final Year IT students to showcase their projects on IoT and Android App Development domains respectively. About 77 students from TE IT and 79 students from BE IT participated in this competition. The Android App domain projects were showcased on the first day i.e. October 22, 2019 and the IoT domain projects were showcased on the second day i.e. October 23, 2019. The faculty in charge for the subject and students organized and participated in this programme very enthusiastically giving working models of where and how IoT can be used in our day today life. The competition was judged in 2 rounds.

Dice Bootcamp

Date: October 05th, 2019 | Venue: ISDI Bulls Center lower parel

The IETE Students’ Forum in collaboration with the Entrepreneurship Development Cell of SIES Graduate School of Technology, Nerul organized a Boot Camp on Design Thinking and Data Science at the Indian School of Management and Entrepreneurship, One India Bulls, Parel. In its second iteration, this boot camp was aimed at providing students with new insights and career opportunities after their Engineering. This event was held on October 05, 2019.There were total 118 students from BE, across all years of engineering, 7 volunteers from TE-IT and 11 faculty members. This session included career guidance and a peek into the various courses offered at ISDI and ISME, Mumbai. The introductory session was followed by the session on Design Thinking by Prof. Amit Kundal, ISDI Mumbai.
